Your customers are mobile, and so should your billing - Hummingbill supports multiple types of invoices:

  • SMS Shortcodes
  • Phone call
  • Email

No matter the type of billing or revenue model, Hummingbill has it covered:

  • Daily, Weekly, Monthly, Quarterly and Annual
  • Single and Multi-Subscription
  • Usage-based
  • Lease-to-own
  • Pay-As-You-Go
  • Tiered and Packaged Pricing

Hummingbill simplifies daily billing through smart automation. From calculating bills to delivering receipts, Hummingbill saves businesses hundreds of hours each month. Hummingbill Automates:

  • Bill Calculation
  • Bill & Receipt Delivery
  • Payment Reconciliation
  • Balance Notifications
  • Subscription Changes & Dunning

  • Visa and Mastercard
